# SmartSolar ☀️

**SolarSmart** is an AI-powered platform for planning, financing, and maintaining rooftop solar systems. It helps users optimize solar panel placement, estimate energy production, find affordable green financing options, and monitor efficiency loss due to dust and weather conditions — all in a single dashboard. SolarSmart makes solar adoption easier, more efficient, and cost-effective while reducing carbon emissions.

---

## Features
- Optimal rooftop solar panel placement recommendations using satellite imagery  
- Solar energy production estimation and savings forecast  
- Green financing matcher with ROI and break-even calculations  
- Dust and weather-based efficiency monitoring with cleaning alerts  
- Unified dashboard showing performance, savings, and maintenance insights  

---

## Target Users
- Homeowners  
- Small businesses  
- Solar installation companies  
- Financial institutions offering green loans  
- Local governments promoting renewable energy  

---

## Problem Statement
Many households and small businesses struggle with solar adoption due to high upfront costs, poor rooftop panel placement, and efficiency loss from dust accumulation. This results in wasted money, lower energy output, and slower renewable energy adoption, especially in developing regions.

---

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React / Next.js  
- **Backend:** Node.js / FastAPI  
- **Database:** Firebase / MongoDB  
- **AI / ML:** Python + TensorFlow / Scikit-learn  
- **APIs:** Maps/Satellite API, Weather & Dust API, Solar Radiation API  

---

## How It Works
1. Users select their rooftop location or upload roof dimensions.  
2. AI analyzes sun exposure and shading to recommend optimal panel layout.  
3. Financial quiz matches users with local green loan options and calculates ROI.  
4. Dust and weather data estimate efficiency loss and generate cleaning alerts.  
5. All insights are displayed in a unified dashboard with forecasts and savings.  

---

## Why SolarSmart Is Innovative
SolarSmart combines solar system design, financing, and maintenance into one platform. It empowers users to make data-driven decisions, increases solar adoption, improves energy efficiency, reduces electricity costs, and lowers carbon emissions.
